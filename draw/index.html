🎟️ Fair Draw

UVS + drand 🟢

Cup final / concert: N seats raffled among M applicants. Every loser can prove they weren't quietly dropped, and that no winner was hand-picked — instead of taking the organiser's word that "it was a random draw."

Set up the draw

The applicant list and a secret seed are committed first; the winner-picking randomness comes from a drand round that hasn't happened yet — so nobody can be steered in.

drand round —
Winner score: SHA-256( SHA-256(serverSeed : drandRandomness) : applicantId ). Winners = the N highest scores. Re-fetch the drand round from any mirror (link above), recompute every applicant's score, and you reproduce the exact winner set — and see your own rank. Because the list and the seed were committed before the drand round existed, nobody could be added, dropped, or steered after the fact.
